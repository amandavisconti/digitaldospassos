STRIKING WAITERS ASK AID OF WOMEN



This headline might refer to the 1912-1913 New York City strike of waiters at high-profile restaurants; the strike was organized by the Wobblies (Industrial Workers of the World).

Presented here is one of many New York Times articles on the strike: "Waiters' Strike Ill-Timed: Just When High Cost of Living Ruins Restaurants".
